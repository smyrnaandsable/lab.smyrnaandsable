Case Study — Sustainability

9 Boyoz = 1 Tree

A data pipeline connecting commercial sales performance to environmental reforestation efforts. For every 9 units of İzmir's signature pastry sold, one tree is planted. The project translates a local product into a measurable sustainability metric.

Python Data Simulation SQL Data Visualization
53.6%
Chocolate Boyoz identified as the market leader — contributing more than half of all tree plantings in the "Boyoz Forest" dataset.
Technical Execution
  • Engine: Realistic sales data simulated using Python and Pandas across multiple product variants and time periods.
  • Transformation: SQL logic applied to convert raw sales figures into tree planting metrics based on the 9:1 ratio.
  • Strategic Insight: Chocolate Boyoz identified as the dominant contributor — 53.6% of total environmental impact — informing production and marketing prioritization.
